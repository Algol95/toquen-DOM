Xylophone Virtual Instrument

Instructions

Play the xylophone using the mouse or your keyboard.

Mouse Click

Click on the keys to play notes (C, D, E, F, G, A, B).

Keyboard Keys

Press letter keys (C, D, E, F, G, A, B) for natural notes.

Sharp Notes

Hold Shift + Key (C, D, F, G, A) for sharp notes (C#, D#, F#, G#, A#).

Create your own melody and have fun!
